description: Learn how to start a new project in Personal.
---

## Steps

1. Log in to your Personal dashboard.
2. Click the "+ New Project" button on the top right.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Choose your preferred settings or use defaults.
5. Click "Create" to finish.

All your projects will appear in the Projects list on the dashboard. For more details, see our step-by-step guide in the Guides section.
