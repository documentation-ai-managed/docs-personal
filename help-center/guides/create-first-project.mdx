## Overview

This guide walks you through adding your first project in Personal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access dashboard">
    Log in to your Personal account and go to the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start project wizard">
    Click on the "+ New Project" button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter project details">
    Fill out the name, description, and any basic details. Leave advanced options at default if unsure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish setup">
    Press "Create"; your new project appears on your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
