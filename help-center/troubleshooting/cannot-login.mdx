---
title: Cannot Log In
description: Fix login issues with your Personal account.
---

## Symptoms

- Incorrect password error
- Account not recognized

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Make sure you are using the right email and password. Try typing them again carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions. Check your inbox for a reset link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If you still can't log in, email support@personal.com for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
